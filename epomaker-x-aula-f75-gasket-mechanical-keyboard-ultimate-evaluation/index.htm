Introduction to the Collaboration

The collaboration between EPOMAKER and Aula, a renowned name in the custom keyboard community, has resulted in the F75 Gasket model that emphasizes a gasket-mounted design. Gasket mounting is highly regarded among keyboard enthusiasts because it enhances sound dampening and typing comfort, creating a softer, more refined keystroke. This partnership combines EPOMAKER’s manufacturing expertise with Aula’s design philosophy, leading to a keyboard that balances aesthetics, functionality, and durability.

Design and Build Quality

Materials and Construction

Feature Description
Frame Aluminum alloy top plate offers rigidity and a premium feel, reducing flex and enhancing durability.
Case High-quality plastic with a matte finish, resistant to fingerprints and scratches.
Gasket Mounting Silicone gaskets between the top plate and PCB absorb vibrations, providing a softer typing experience.
Keycap Material PBT double-shot keycaps ensure longevity and resist shine over prolonged use.
Weight Approximately 2.8 kg, making it stable on desktops during intense gaming sessions.

Design Aesthetics

The F75 Gasket features a sleek, minimalist aesthetic with a focus on clean lines and understated elegance. It is available in multiple colorways, including classic black, silver, and custom limited editions. The keyboard’s compact 75% layout strikes a balance between functionality and space-saving, making it suitable for both professional setups and gaming rigs.

Features and Technical Specifications

  • Layout: 75% layout with dedicated arrow keys and function row.
  • Switch Compatibility: Hot-swappable PCB supports a wide range of MX-style switches, including tactile, linear, and clicky types.
  • Keycaps: PBT double-shot, OEM profile, with dye-sublimated legends on some variants.
  • Backlighting: RGB per-key backlit with customizable lighting effects via software or onboard controls.
  • Connectivity: Wired via USB-C, with support for NKRO and 6-key rollover.
  • Firmware: Open-source firmware compatible with QMK and VIA, allowing extensive customization.
  • Additional Features: DIP switches for layout customization, adjustable feet for ergonomic positioning, and detachable cable for portability.

Performance Analysis

Typing Experience

The gasket-mounted design significantly impacts the typing feel. Users report a softer, more cushioned keystroke with reduced noise levels compared to traditional plate-mounted keyboards. The silicone gaskets effectively dampen vibrations, resulting in a sound profile that is less sharp and more refined, ideal for shared workspaces or quiet environments.

Switch options play a crucial role in the overall experience. The hot-swappable PCB allows users to experiment with switches like Gateron, Cherry MX, or Kailh, customizing tactility, sound, and actuation force. The keyboard’s stabilizers are factory-lubricated, providing smooth keypresses and minimizing rattle.

Gaming and Productivity

Thanks to its compact layout and stable build, the F75 Gasket performs excellently in gaming scenarios. The dedicated arrow keys and function row facilitate quick commands, while the robust construction prevents flexing during intense gameplay. For productivity tasks, the customizable key mapping and software support streamline workflows and reduce keystroke fatigue.

Sound Profile and Acoustics

One of the standout features of the F75 Gasket is its acoustic performance. The gasket mounting absorbs keystroke impact, resulting in a mellow, muted sound that is often preferred for office environments or late-night gaming. According to user data and sound tests conducted in 2025, the F75 Gasket produces decibel levels around 55-60 dB on average, notably quieter than plate-mounted counterparts, which often exceed 65 dB.

Pricing and Market Position

As of 2025, the EPOMAKER x Aula F75 Gasket retails at approximately $180-$220 USD, positioning it competitively within the high-end mechanical keyboard segment. Its price reflects the quality of materials, gasket mounting technology, and extensive customization options. Compared to other gasket-mounted keyboards like the Drop CTRL or Varmilo VA87M, the F75 offers a compelling balance of features, size, and price.
