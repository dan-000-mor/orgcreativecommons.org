Which couples from Married At First Sight UK 2025 are still together?

- March 21, 2026

Finding long-lasting love on E4’s Married At First Sight UK is rare, but it does happen – as one happy couple proved in the latest series reunion.

However, they are the only couple that has stayed together from the 2025 series as one split during final vows and three have said their goodbyes after the cameras stopped rolling.

The first reunion – filmed eight weeks after the show wrapped – aired back in November, where one couple announced their split, with more break-ups following in the weeks and months afterwards.

All the MAFS UK couples from the 2024 series had broken up by the time the six-month reunion filming had come around, including the strongest pair: Lacey and Nathan.

Rebecca and Bailey split up

The exes faced an awkward reunion.

(Channel 4)

Rebecca (Bec) 和 Bailey 在实验结束后，各自选择了不同的道路。 尽管他们尝试在外部维持关系，但他们最终决定结束这段感情。 在他们分手后不久，Bec 在 Instagram 上写道：”为了回答所有问题……是的，他犯了错误。”

Bailey 分享了他对这段关系结束的 “心碎”。 他在 Instagram 上写道：”我们尽了最大的努力在实验后生活，尽管我们在外部度过了很多美好的时光，但不幸的是，这段关系结束了。 我仍然对 Bec 怀有爱，并且感谢我们共同经历的精彩关系。 我完全爱上了 Bec，这确实是一段真挚的爱情故事。”

他补充道：”我不会说谎，看着我们在屏幕上重新坠入爱河，这对我来说非常困难，而且我现在仍在为此挣扎。 但我不会改变任何事情！”

Rebecca 和 Bailey 在 Married At First Sight UK 中说 “是”。

(Channel 4)

“不幸的是，我也必须处理自 Married At First Sight 开始以来，不断流传的各种虚假在线指控，这对我来说非常困难。 我想明确地说明，我从未在任何时候背叛过 Bec。 我没有很好地处理我们的分手，对此我向 Bec 诚挚道歉，但我始终忠于这段关系。”

“I’m not perfect, I don’t claim to be and I take responsibility for my part in why the relationship didn’t work. Making the decision to end things was heartbreaking and not a decision that I took lightly. I’m still heartbroken, but I wish Bec all the best and I always will.”

The couple had said “yes” to love in the MAFS vow renewals, but there was a question mark over where to live outside of the experiment. In Liverpool, the bride had a beautiful home surrounded by her family and had built her business with loyal customers. In Brighton, the groom had his daughter, Blue, with his ex-partner, Lulu.

Rebecca and Bailey were torn over where to live after Married At First Sight UK.

(Channel 4)

However, Bec ruled out that the distance wasn’t the reason they split up. She wrote on Instagram stories: “We filmed the reunion in June and we split the month after, when we were away for the weekend at a festival with some of the other cast members. The breakup completely broke me. I won’t drag Bailey’s name through the mud but there is no chance of us getting back together.”

Further details have since emerged that Bailey had called time on the relationship twice – once on a trip to Dublin, and for the second time at the festival. A devastated Bec had left the festival early, and Bailey had then slept with the best friend of fellow MAFS star April.

La mayor parte de la dramatización de la segunda reunión se centró en el incidente, ya que Bec chocó con April afirmando que había “rompido el código de chicas” al no llamarla inmediatamente al respecto. Pero April dijo que se había encontrado en una posición imposible y que había dado a Bailey 48 horas para que le comunicara la noticia, pero cuando no lo hizo, April le había contado a Bec ella misma.

April se vio reducida a las lágrimas por un encuentro con Bec en la reunión.

(Channel 4)

Bailey llegó a la reunión afirmando que todavía amaba a Bec y con la esperanza de que pudieran arreglar las cosas, pero le dijo que no había ninguna posibilidad después de descubrir que había tenido una relación con la amiga de April apenas cinco horas después de que ella dejara el festival. Bailey argumentó que no habían sido una pareja en ese momento, por lo que no necesitaba contarle.

Mientras Bec lo culpaba por su ruptura, Bailey tenía otra bomba que compartir en la segunda reunión. Le dijo al grupo que, después de grabar la primera reunión, en el taxi que salía del programa, Bec había admitido que había estado enviando mensajes a su ex durante el programa, lo que, según él, había destruido su confianza en ella y había provocado la ruptura.

Bec más tarde publicó en Instagram sobre por qué había tenido una reacción tan fuerte a April, pero dijo que estaba “en paz” con la experiencia, ya que había pasado a una nueva relación. Ahora está en una relación con el futbolista de Liverpool, Lily Woodham.

La pareja había pasado de un comienzo incierto a una de las parejas más fuertes en el experimento de MAFS. Después de la renovación de votos, Bec escribió: “La experiencia que cambió mi vida, para siempre… Yo dije que sí.”

Bailey wrote: “Well… that was emotional to say the least! The perfect way to wrap up our experience and take our first steps outside of the bubble… together! From a wedding day where it looked like we might not make it 10 minutes, to 10 weeks later our biggest worry being how we’d manage living miles apart, who would’ve thought it! I came in with my heart on my sleeve and loved every minute of our journey.”

They made it to the first reunion as a couple where Bec even hinted that a move to Hove could be on the cards for her and Bailey got excited about starting a family together.

The former couple couldn’t decide where to live after Married At First Sight UK.

(Channel 4)

Before the big reveal, Bec spoke about the difficulty of keeping it a secret. She told Yahoo UK: “It’s so hard. I have people stop me in the supermarket and talk about MAFS, and then they ask me, and people will comment saying ‘she’s not wearing a ring.’ I don’t wear any rings at all, except when going out… obviously, I work in a clinic, so I can never have rings on.

“People always try to pick it apart and go, ‘Well, this might be happening, so she’s with him or she’s not with him,’ and we can’t say either way. I think that’s fun for everyone because everyone gets to go through it in real time with us as the show goes on.

“But it is hard to navigate because we’ve obviously come back from the show earlier in the year and carried on with our lives. Some people [are] together, some people [are] not together. So it has been really strange to navigate even before the show started airing, and even more difficult now because there are so many eyes on us.”

Davide and Keye broke up over a dating app row

It was a frosty reunion for Keye and Davide.

(Channel 4)

Davide and Keye split up over Keye going on dating app Grindr to speak to strangers, with Davide saying it “broke” his trust.

It led to a fraught encounter at the second reunion dinner party, as both looked devastated and rowed over whose fault the breakdown of their relationship had been.

But showing the care and maturity that they had been known for during much of the series, the exes then managed to bury the hatchet at the dinner table as Keye reassured Davide that he had been a great partner and they hugged it out.

Keye and Davide had fireworks at their Married At First Sight UK wedding.

(Channel 4)

In a lengthy message after their split, Davide wrote on Instagram: “In light of mine and Keye’s relationship status being shared, I just wanted to speak on the situation personally.

“My time on MAFS was honestly one of the most life-changing experiences of my life. From the start I showed up as my true self, I was vulnerable, open, and completely genuine. I went into the experiment with an open heart and a real hope of finding love and I did.

“Before the experiment I had already done a lot of work on myself, especially around my boundaries and trust. Trust has always been one of my biggest boundaries and I came into this experience knowing it was something I would never compromise again.

“The night in question (this happened 5 months ago), I had a gut feeling something wasn’t right. Out of my own insecurity I created a profile on Grindr to check whether Keye was on the app only to find that my gut feeling was correct.

Davide and Keye shared a kiss at the final vow ceremony on MAFS UK.

(Channel 4)

“That moment was painful and it made the trust between us break in a way I couldn’t move past. That’s why when it was broken, it was something I simply could not overcome. I won’t lie I was deeply hurt and shocked but with time, I’ve healed and found peace.

“Even through the pain I don’t regret being part of this journey. This experience pushed me to grow in ways I never expected. It taught me patience, communication and what it truly means to choose yourself after heartbreak.”

He added: “I don’t hold any bitterness towards Keye, he has taken accountability for his actions and I respect that. I genuinely wish him peace and happiness. Sometimes love doesn’t end the way we hope but it still leaves behind lessons that shape you for the better, for that I’ll always be grateful.”

Davide and Keye had found love on Married At First Sight UK.

(Channel 4)

Keye was the first to speak on their split and own up to going on a dating app which led to the end of their relationship.

He wrote on Instagram: “This is incredibly difficult to share, but I have always believed in being honest, even when it is uncomfortable.

“In the early hours of a Sunday morning, while Davide was on a flight, I downloaded Grindr. I want to be clear that I was not looking to cheat, hook up, or find someone else. I was in a dark and fragile place and reached for something unhealthy because I did not know how else to cope. What I was seeking had nothing to do with intimacy. It was an attempt to escape emotions I could not sit with, and I reached for substances when what I really needed was support.

“None of this excuses my actions. I understand how it looked and I understand why it hurt. Even without sexual intentions, I crossed a line and broke trust. I hate that Davide believed I was looking for someone else, because that was never the truth. But I fully accept the impact.

“When Davide found my profile through his, it cost me my marriage. That is something I carry with me every day. I have nothing but love for him. What we shared was real, both in the experiment and in the months after. He deserves the absolute best, and I am devastated that my choices caused him pain.”

Davide has split from Keye after Married At First Sight UK.

(Channel 4)

He added: “Since then, I have been doing the work; therapy, accountability, and learning healthier ways to cope, so I never return to destructive patterns. It has not been easy, but it has been necessary.

“Looking back, I see how lost I was. The emotions are still raw, but I am learning to face them without running, numbing, or repeating the behaviors that hurt others and myself. Now is not the time to unpack our relationship. What matters is acknowledging the truth, the love, the mistakes, and choosing to move forward with honesty and growth.”

They had fireworks on their wedding day, and they were a fan favourite pairing throughout the series.

Their picture-perfect relationship was rocked by the Home Stay week, where Keye was uncomfortable in the cat café and joked about Greggs. This didn’t go down well with viewers at home.

Understanding, Davide told Yahoo UK: “It was a tough moment for both of us. Emotions were running high, and we were still learning how to communicate. I understood where his frustration came from — the dislike for cats and small spaces made him feel very overwhelmed, but I also knew it was a chance for us to learn from it. Every couple has moments like that; what matters is how you move forward together.”

Davide and Keye were one of the strongest Married At First Sight UK couples.

(Channel 4)

Moving on, the couple got back to a good place ahead of the final vows. They committed to each other to see if it would work in the outside world. The couple were still going strong at the reunion airing on Thursday.

Asked about his biggest worry about the relationship, Davide told Yahoo UK: “My biggest worry was whether we’d both be ready for the outside world, because that’s when things get real. Inside the experiment, it’s intense but contained. Outside, it’s about balance, trust, and showing up every day.”

Leisha and Reiss broke up but follow each other

Leisha and Reiss caught up at the final reunion.

(Channel 4)

Leisha and Reiss had a rocky relationship as late arrivals on MAFS.

The rollercoaster turbulence of their relationship continued, and their final date ended in tears after Leisha was unhappy that Reiss had chosen a theme park as their destination. Outspoken Leisha was happily shouting she loved him, while a more reserved Reiss said he was heading that way but not ready to say it.

Their vow renewals were left on a cliffhanger with Reiss yet to deliver his verdict on the marriage until part two, where he had the chance to say “yes” to love.

Leisha and Reiss said yes at the end of Married At First Sight UK.

(Channel 4)

Leisha and Reiss follow each other on Instagram but they confirmed in the reunion show on Thursday that their relationship was over. Candidly, Leisha admitted she was “gutted” that they had split.

It was reported that his TOWIE ex, Dani Imbert, ended speculation that she had got back with him after MAFS in an Instagram Q&A too.

Leah and Leigh split

Things are definitely over for Leah and Leigh.

(Channel 4)

Leah and Leigh were the will-they-won’t-they couple of the series. It was a shocker that the pair managed to make it all the way to the final vows, where they traded barbs instead of sweet nothings.

On arrival, Leigh had a dramatic black bow tied to the back of her white mini dress. Last year, bride Amy had chosen to wear black to declare her marriage to Luke was over.

Leigh and Leah ended their romance on Married At First Sight UK.

(Channel 4)

When Leigh read out her vows, Leah openly admitted that she “disagreed” with what was being said about their relationship. Their marriage went up in flames with a fiery exchange from both parties.

Leah then began dating footballer Maz Pacheco. Leigh claimed that Leah’s new girlfriend was the “girl in the bar” that she had got upset over her talking to on the show.

She wrote on Instagram stories: “Naturally, I was shocked as I’d watched myself in the experiment be persuaded that I’d overreacted for expressing my concerns at the time and had nothing to worry about.”
