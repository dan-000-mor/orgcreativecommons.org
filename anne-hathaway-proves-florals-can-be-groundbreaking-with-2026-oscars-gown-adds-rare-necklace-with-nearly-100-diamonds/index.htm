Anne Hathaway Proves Florals Can Be Groundbreaking with 2026 Oscars Gown, Adds Rare Necklace with Nearly 100 Diamonds

- March 23, 2026

NEED TO KNOW

  • Anne Hathaway arrived at the 2026 Oscars wearing a show-stopping floral gown and diamonds
  • The actress appeared to lean into The Devil Wears Prada with her Valentino gown, which was later seen when she took the stage with Anna Wintour
  • Hathaway is reprising her role as Andrea Sachs in the new Devil Wears Prada sequel out May 1

Did someone say Runway?

Fan around the world are anticipating a stylish press tour to come out of The Devil Wears Prada 2, which is releasing on May 1. But for now, we’ve got its main star, Anne Hathaway, delivering one of the best fashion moments this year the 2026 Oscars.

On March 15, the actress 43, returned to the ceremony after a 12-year hiatus, dressed in a show-stopping floral Valentino gown that leaned into the iconic “Florals? For spring? Groundbreaking,” quote delivered by Meryl Streep’s Miranda Priestly 20 years ago.

Hathaway’s ensemble of course centered around her printed strapless mermaid gown, which also featured a train. She accessorized with long black gloves and a stunning display of Bulgari diamonds.

Anne Hathaway looking glamorous in a floral strapless gown and Bulgari diamond sat the 2026 Oscars
Credit: Kevin Mazur/Getty

According to a press release from the brand, Hathaway, one of the jeweler’s brand ambassadors, wore the Neoclassical Starlight High Jewelry necklace boasting a rare 8.02 carat pear-cut Fancy Vivid Yellow diamond surrounded by a constellation nearly 100 pear, round and step-cut diamonds, bringing the total diamond weight to over 35 carats. It was crafted over 850 hours.

In addition to the neckpiece, Hathaway also sparkled in Bulgari’s Daphne’s Laurel earrings set with Fancy Intense Yellow diamonds and white diamonds, as well as two platinum rings set with yellow diamonds.

Her hair was styled in an elegant half-up style teamed with a peachy glam and shimmery lip.

Later in the night, Hathaway and Vogue icon Anna Wintour appeared onstage to present the awards for costume design and achievement in hair and makeup. The appearance was not without a skit inspired by Streep’s Priestly (who is widely understood to have been inspired by Wintour) and Hathaway’s Andrea Sachs.

Anna Wintour and Anne Hathaway presenting at the 2026 Oscars
Credit: Patrick T. Fallon / AFP via Getty

Devil Wears Prada 2 will star the returning Hathaway, Streep, Emily Blunt, Stanley Tucci, Tracie Thoms and Tibor Feldman, plus Kenneth Branagh, Lucy Liu, Justin Theroux, B.J. Novak, Pauline Chalamet, Patrick Brammall, Caleb Hearon, Helen J. Shen and Conrad Ricamora. David Frankel and Aline Brosh McKenna have returned to direct and write the screenplay, respectively.

En una entrevista para Vogue publicada el 1 de febrero, Hathaway dijo que está más emocionada de ver a los fans del clásico rom-com de culto involucrarse en la diversión elegante.

“Espero que la gente recuerde lo divertida que fue la experiencia de ponerse rosa brillante y ir a ver Barbie. Espero que todos se pongan su atuendo favorito aprobado por Miranda Priestly y se diviertan.”
