Sydney Sweeney Poses in Blush Pink Balletcore Lingerie Paired With Lace-Up Stiletto Slippers

- March 23, 2026

The Gist

  • Sydney Sweeney took style cues from ballerinas while announcing her latest lingerie launch on March 18.
  • The Euphoria actress posed in a blush pink and white lace balletcore bra and underwear, as well as lace-up stiletto slippers.
  • Sweeney also modeled a plunging white corset ahead of her Syrn Seductress release on March 24.

After cycling through her four different Syrn lingerie personas over the past few months, Sydney Sweeney is back to square one—and reinventing her Seductress character with a brand new launch. Unlike the sultry black lace designs and masquerade ball-inspired masks of her previous launch, the Euphoria actress put a ballerina-approved twist on boudoir dressing while posing in a blush pink balletcore lingerie set.

Sweeney teased her upcoming Seductress Drop 2 launch in several Syrn Instagram posts on Wednesday, counting down to its March 24 release date. In one photo carousel, The Housemaid actress could be seen leaning back on a red velvet bench, holding a red rose in one hand as she showed off a light pink satin halterneck balconette bra. The plunging style featured unlined sheer white lace bra cups and the same lace wrapping around her waistband and back.

Sydney Sweeney attends the premiere of “Christy” during the 2025 Toronto International Film Festival at Princess of Wales Theatre on September 5, 2025
Credit: Getty Images

Sweeney paired the halterneck look with a miniskirt-inspired white lace garter belt, which fanned out with white lace on the sides. Strappy white garters and white lace underwear finished off her lingerie set. In lieu of pointe ballet slippers, Sweeney accessorized with pointy-toed white slingback stilettos featuring a sheer white ruched lace material. Light pink satin ribbons latched up her legs.

Sydney Sweeney attends “The Housemaid” New York screening at 787 Seventh Ave on December 2, 2025
Credit: Getty Images

The Anyone But You actress could be seen sporting a darker blonde hair color with brighter golden ends on her side-parted bombshell curls, mirroring the “bronde” color she recently sported for Syrn’s girl-next-door-coded Comfy collection launch. A multi-strand beaded necklace, smoky black eyeliner, and a rose pink lipstick finished off her look.

Sydney Sweeney attends the “Christy” premiere during 2025 AFI FEST at TCL Chinese Theater on October 25, 2025
Credit: Getty Images

Meanwhile, Sweeney slipped into a plunging white corset for another Instagram carousel, sitting on the top of a red velvet movie theater seat in the promo photos. The star paired the surprisingly bridal look with sheer white mesh opera gloves, white lace underwear, and a single multi-strand garter on her leg as she lounged on a fluffy white coat.
